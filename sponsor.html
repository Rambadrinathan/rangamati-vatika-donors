What Sponsors Receive

Naming is just the beginning. Every sponsor becomes part of the campus community with tangible rights and ongoing connection.

Permanent Naming Rights

Hand-crafted stone or brass dedication plaque at the entrance of your sponsored space, inscribed in both English and Bengali. Choose your honorific: Mata, Pitrideb, Bhrata, Adorini, Sant, Mahatma, or Acharya.

Nomination Rights

Swajan Griha sponsors can nominate an elder for lifetime residence. Atithi Kutir sponsors receive 2 weeks per year of guest stay for themselves or their nominees.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I sponsor a space in memory of someone who has passed?
Absolutely. Many sponsors choose to dedicate a space in loving memory of a parent, elder, or mentor. The dedication plaque will bear any name and honorific you choose. This is one of the most common and meaningful forms of sponsorship.
Can I visit the campus before sponsoring?
Yes. We welcome potential sponsors to visit RangaMati and see the land, the river, and the construction progress firsthand. Contact us to arrange a visit, and we will host you personally.
Is this a real estate investment?
No. This is a philanthropic sponsorship with naming rights, not a property purchase. You are sponsoring the construction of a community space, and your name will be permanently associated with it. The campus is owned and operated by KarmYog for 21st Century Foundation, an AOP registered in 2008 under the West Bengal Society Registration Act (XXVI), 1961.
What if I want to sponsor an entire ashram?
Each of the four ashrams can carry a single sponsor's name at the entrance, etched in stone in English and Bengali. For ashram-level sponsorship, please speak with us directly. This is our highest tier and comes with the full Founder's Circle privileges plus additional recognition.
Can I nominate someone for residence even if I'm not a Swajan Griha sponsor?
Nomination rights for elder residence are specific to Swajan Griha sponsors. However, the campus welcomes applications from elders in need of dignified community living, and we will work to accommodate deserving individuals regardless of sponsorship status.
How long does the naming last?
Permanently. The dedication plaques are part of the building structure itself, designed to endure for generations. Your name is not on a temporary donor board, it is etched into the entrance of the space you sponsored.
