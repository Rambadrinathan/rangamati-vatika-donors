The Campus

An Intergenerational Campus on the Ganges

KarmYog Vatika at RangaMati is spread across 97 kathas on the banks of the Ganges in rural West Bengal. Four Named Ashrams, each a self-contained community, surround the Dharma Mata Shakti Devi Temple Complex.

  • Dharma Temple Complex — construction commenced January 2026
  • Performing Arts Centers & Science Labs in every ashram
  • Community Dining Halls with farm-to-table gardens
  • Ganges-side meditation decks and learning spaces
  • 150 learners, 60 elders, and visiting scholars living together
